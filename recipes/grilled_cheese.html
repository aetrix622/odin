Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 slices american cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter, divided
  • 1 tsp oregano

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat.

  2. Assemble your sandwich. Place the cheese between the bread and sprinkle with some oregano.

  3. Add half the butter to the pan and melt. Place the sandwich on top of the melted butter and grill for 90 seconds

  4. Remove the sandwich. Melt the rest of the butter, then place the ungrilled side of the sandwich face down on top of the butter. Grill for an additional 90 seconds

  5. Remove from heat and slice in half diagonally. Serve immediately.
