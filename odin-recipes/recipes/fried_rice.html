Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for fried rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas, frozen
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Make the rice ahead of time

    Rinse the rice well. Add to 4 cups salted boiling water. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes or until water is absorbed.

    Refrigerate the cooked rice overnight.

  2. Add 1 tbsp butter to a hot pan. Scramble the egg, then set aside.

  3. Add 1 tbsp butter to the pan. Add the carrots, peas and onions. Cook for 5 minutes on medium-high heat until softened.

  4. Turn heat to high. Add the remaining butter and rice. Stir fry for 5 minutes.

  5. Remove from heat. Add sesame oil and egg. Stir to combine. Serve immediately.
