Hot Dog

Description

This is a recipe for making a hotdog.

Ingredients

  • 1 hotdog bun
  • 1 hotdog
  • mustard
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat.

  2. Add the hotdog to the pan. Cook, turning occasionally, until browned.

  3. Place the cooked hotdog betwixt the fluffy halves of the hotdog bun.

  4. Apply ketchup and mustard atop the hotdog if desired.

  5. Enjoy!
